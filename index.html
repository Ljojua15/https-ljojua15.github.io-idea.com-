Project Idea

We want to hold the first NFT Poker Tournament.

To participate in the tournament you need to buy poker combinations on our official Opensea page.

Every poker combination has its value for both NFT and real life use.

We want to set a precedent for NFT's real-life use. Especially for poker lovers.
